Story

Woodas - Wood Art. Fun. Eco-friendly.

We appreciate the beauty of different type of woods. Different type of woods got their own unique features, such as their own grain, odor, color or even hardness. Every species is unique and has its own characteristics.

Apart from appreciate the amazing features of different woods, we have gone one step further. We understand that there is a very serious problem on deforestation and we don't want to chop down even an extra tree to create things for our own enjoyment. We need to protect the planet. Therefore, we have chosen to used recycled and unwanted wood to build Woodas in order to make it environmental-friendly.

Let's take a look on the unique characteristics of Woodas material first:

We emphasize a lot on eco-friendly production as we understand that protecting the environment should be on the top of the agenda for all the human. Therefore, we collect unwanted wood furniture in the market and also cooperate with different factories to collect their unwanted scrap wood. Then we process them and transform them into this amazing little toy!

Every piece of Woodas rock is hand-crafted by our wood smith. Hand-crafting wood takes time but it can pay attention to so much more small details. Therefore, when you look at each one in detail, you will discover that each one has at least slight differences. Every piece of Woodas is unique and is the heart and soul of our wood smith.

It works just like aroma oil or essential oil. The smell of the natural wood can help you to relax, concentrate and stay focus. It can also help to awaken yourself when you feel sleepy. That's why apart from a toy, it is also a perfect decoration on your desk at home or in the office.

Playing with Woodas is a great wait to train your brain and creativity. You can construct Woodas in millions of different ways. Just use your creativity. Also, you can simply just stack it up ! Your choice! No matter which way you are playing with Woodas, it is an amazing way to train your mind, your patience, as well as your creativity. Besides, many of our trial player said this is a perfect game for the ME Time.

You can always challenge yourself with 2 set or even 3 set of Woodas. You can stack and create different shapes or even compete with your friend. You can compete with your friend to see who can stack higher and our team record is 16 pieces! See if you can break our record !

The Woodas's mission is to create fun things while protecting our planet. One day, our team has passed through a garbage station and discovered many people threw away their wooden furniture even though they are still quite new. That's why our team have come up with the idea of reusing the unwanted wood furniture to create the fun and interesting wooden toy - Woodas. Our team hope we can raise the attention to protect our planet. We can all have fun while protecting our lovely planet !

Risks and challenges

We have noticed that the logistics recently takes quite a long time probably because of the pandemic. Because we have noticed this issue, we have already found several different logistics company as alternatives. Also, we compare the price and time of shipment between them to get the best choices. We will keep on looking for better company in order to get the best deal with faster shipment.
